Files to delete manually from project root:
- .modified
- tsconfig.tsbuildinfo
- phionix.sql (if you moved it to db/)
- icon.png (move to public/)

Run the following PowerShell commands to remove them manually:
Remove-Item -Path .\.modified -Force -ErrorAction SilentlyContinue
Remove-Item -Path .\tsconfig.tsbuildinfo -Force -ErrorAction SilentlyContinue
Move-Item -Path .\phionix.sql -Destination .\db\phionix.sql -Force
Move-Item -Path .\icon.png -Destination .\public\icon.png -Force
